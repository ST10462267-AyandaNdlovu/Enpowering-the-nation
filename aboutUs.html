This company, founded by Precious Radebe, provides skills training and upskilling programs for domestic workers and gardeners. The training enhances their employability, market value, and enables them to start their own businesses. Employers can also upskill their employees through these programs. Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves and to provide more marketable skills.
